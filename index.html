WHO WE ARE

About Dharma Kala Vahini

⸻ ❋ ⸻

Dharma Kala Vahini is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting social welfare, cultural awareness, and community development through various initiatives and events.

Our work bridges the ancient wisdom of our traditions with the needs of modern communities, creating lasting impact through cultural programs, social service, and educational outreach.

🪔

Mission

To serve society through cultural initiatives and social welfare programs.

🌸

Vision

A culturally enriched, harmonious society where traditions uplift communities.

🏺

Values

Dharma, Seva, and Sanskriti — righteousness, service, and culture.
