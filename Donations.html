WHY YOUR SUPPORT MATTERS

Where Your Donation Goes

⸻ ❋ ⸻
🪔
"Every rupee you give lights a lamp of hope in our community."
— Dharma Kala Vahini
  • 🎭
    Cultural Events & Programmes

    Funding classical dance, music, and theatrical performances that preserve our rich Telugu heritage.

  • 🤝
    Community Welfare Activities

    Supporting underprivileged families through food drives, health camps, and financial aid.

  • 📚
    Education & Youth Empowerment

    Scholarships, workshops, and awareness programmes that empower the next generation.

  • 🌸
    Religious & Spiritual Programmes

    Organising jagaram, abhishekam, bhajans and devotional events for the community.

  • 🛡️
    100% Transparent Fund Usage

    Every rupee is fully accounted for. We maintain detailed records and are accountable to all donors.
